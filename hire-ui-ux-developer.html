Frequently Asked Questions on UI/UX Design and Development
Why do I need a UX UI design agency?
Even functionally sound apps fail to engage users if the design is poor. Good design makes an app usable and engaging, all while keeping it most aesthetically appealing. The real value that a UI UX design agency addition brings to your business is helping you create a friendly, user-simple, yet visually stunning product. Here are a few reasons why you need to hire a UI/UX Design Agency for creating successful products:.

Here are the key benefits of UI/UX
  • A UI/UX design agency conducts in-depth research to understand the needs, behaviors and preferences of your target users. This information is leveraged to design a product that meets the user’s needs and expectations leading to higher user satisfaction and engagement.
  • In the long run, hiring a UI/UX design agency is cost-effective. Because a well-designed product increases user engagement and retention resulting in higher ROI.
  • The passionate and energetic team of experienced designers at a UI UX agency will be adept at creating intuitive and user-friendly designs. With their skills, they help you create designs that are aesthetically pleasing, functional and easy to use.
What services do UI/UX design agencies offer?
A UI/UX design agency will do deep research to establish the needs, behaviors and preferences of your target users. Such kinds of information are utilized to design a product that exceeds the user's needs and expectations, leading to greater user satisfaction and engagement. Hiring a UI/UX design agency is cost-effective in the long term. Since a well-designed product increases user engagement and retention, thus higher return on investment.
How do I choose the right UI UX design agency for my business?
Picking up the right UI UX design agency is a strategic choice and one that will directly impact how customers are going to perceive your product. It requires a systemic approach where the track record, competence and culture of the agency have to be checked against business needs and goals. You should begin with analyzing the agency's portfolio and see if they are flexible and capable across a wide range of industries. Consider an agency that looks first at the users and designs experiences serving a purpose. Also remember, while working with any UX design agency, the smoothness of the communication is of key importance. The agency should work actively with you, encouraging feedback and updates on project progression. Consider and seek out client testimonials and reviews to validate reliability and professionalism of the agency. Align their service cost with your budget at the end. Basically, it's all about the right balance between cost and value delivered by the design agency.
How long does it take to complete a UI/UX design project?
There is no specific timeline as different projects have their own unique requirement basis which the duration is evaluated. However, it may normally range from 3 months to 1 year.
Which design tools do you use?
Some of the tools that we are using for our UX UI design include Figma, Adobe XD and Sketch. These enable designing high-fidelity wireframes, prototypes and visual designs that collectively represent the funnel to the final product view. However, Figma is mostly in use for designing user-centric designs. Because Figma operates on the cloud, one of its best features is real-time collaboration between many team members. This, therefore, made it a very good choice for our UI/UX team. We could create interactive prototypes using Figma that gave an opportunity to test user flows and collect client feedback.
