The key components of Node.js architecture are as follows:
1. Requests
2. Node.js Server

How much does it cost to develop an iOS application?
The cost of an iOS app development project depends on several factors, such as the project scope, business objective, man-hours required, app complexity, app category, engagement model, country of the developer, and more. On average, the cost of developing an iOS application from the Asian region is somewhere around $5000 to $100,000, whereas, in the UK or US region, it may cost around $10,000 to $450,000. You may get in touch with our experts to get a ballpark estimate for your iOS mobile application development project.
